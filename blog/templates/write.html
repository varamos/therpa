Glossary

Name : A general name of small molecule (ex: quinacrine).

Date : Accepted or published date (years and month) of each article (YYYY.MM).

PMID : Pubmed identifier (https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed) of each article (ex: 123456). Hyperlink will be formed automatically

Properties : An information of each small molecules (ex: a arcidin derivatives).

Type : A role to PrP and/or PrDs progression (ex: binding to PrP).

Effect_to_PrP : An information of small molecule role to prion protein or abnormal form of prion protein (ex: binding to PrP amyloid in brain).

Effect_to_Diseases_progression : An information of small molecule role to progression of prion diseases (ex: enhanced prion disease transmission).

Materials : An information of materials such as species, cells or tissues in the article to confirm roles of small molecules (ex: Hamster).

Information_of_Materials : An detail information of each material (ex: Syrian hamster (infected 263K strain)).

Treatement : An information of treated amount and methods of small molecule in the article (ex: 100mg/kg (i.p.) ).

Binding_site : A binding information of small moelcues to the prion protein (ex: His-111, between 90-231).

Pubchem : input general name of small molecule. Then, hyperlink to PubChem which is a database for compound will be formed automatically (ex: quinacrine ).

Email or ID : Email, ID or Nickname of person who submit data.
